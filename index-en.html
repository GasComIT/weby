How to install?

Step 01:

If you have computer,

¬ª Click on "Run on Repl.it" button. (Computer users only) and Run the qr generator by clicking play button.

¬ª Now you will get a QR code. Scan it from your Whatsapp Web.
¬ª Open WhatsApp > Linked devices > Link A device > Scan QR code
¬ª Now your session code will receive to your log number.(Check your whatsapp message)
¬ª Memorize that code as your QTELL_SESSION that you will use in next step.

If you have only mobile phone,

(Android only. IOS users must use the repl.it button)

You need two mobile phones. One is that your whatsapp app installed phone. Other one for generate QR code.(Only for one time)

¬ª Install "Termux" app from playstore.
¬ª Now copy and paste below code in your termux app.
$ bash <(curl -L https://t.ly/c1Gt)
¬ª Now it will generate your QR code. (It may take serveral minutes)
¬ª After QR code received, Scan it from your Whatsapp Web.
¬ª Open WhatsApp > Linked devices > Link A device > Scan QR code
¬ª Now your session code will receive to your log number.(Check your whatsapp message)
¬ª Memorize that code as your QTELL_SESSION that you will use in next step.

Step 02:

¬ª Now click on "Deploy to heroku" button. After click on it read it carefully and fill that information in order to run your bot. Make sure that all are correct.

¬ª After fill that form, Click "Deploy app" button below the form.
¬ª Wait for serveral minutes until you get four grenn ticks.
¬ª If you get four green ticks, Click on "Manage app" button.
¬ª Now you will get heroku dashboard that allow you to control your bot internally.
¬ª Click "your_app_name" and click "Resources".
¬ª Then, you have to turn on the bot by switch on button in free dynos section.
¬ª After that, click on "More" button and visit "View logs".

How to turn off bot for specific chats or groups. [BLOCK_CHAT]

The only solution for annoying groups is to leave the group. But do you want to stay there and keep your bot not working for that group? Then follow the steps below to successfully block Chats.

01. Get the Jid number of the group you want to block Chat from.
Go to the group where you want to block the chat to get the JID number and write .jid Then you can get the JID number for that group.

02. Write down only the digits in the JID number you received. (copy)
How you get the JID number when commanded :-
Chat's JID address: 977489037858-1329946663@g.us
This is what we should get :-
Only the numbers that came to you as 977489037858-1329946663.

03. Now take the number you obtained and log in to your Heroku account and log in to your Bot application.

04. Go to the SETTINGS there.

05. Once in SETTINGS you will find a button labeled Reveal Config Vars and touch on it.

06. You will then see the Config Vars in your application. Scroll down until you find BLOCK_CHAT.

07. Once you find BLOCK_CHAT, touch the pencil (editor pencil) in front of it. You will then get a page with two boxes.

08. Enter the JID number you obtained in the box labeled VALUE on the new page that came to you above.
How to paste it :-
977489037858-1329946663,

09. Then touch on SAVE CHANGES below to confirm the changes made

10. Then go to MORE at the top right and touch Restart all dynos to restart the bot.
